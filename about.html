About the Artist

Elise Pringle took to pen and paper from an extremely young age. "As soon as she could hold a pencil she was pretty much drawing non-stop" Said her Mother. Born and raised in the San Francisco Bay Area Elise attended College of Marin for her AA in Studio Art California State University East Bay for her BFA in Graphic Design. Elise was raised in a broken home and tragically lost her two brothers at the age of 24, this pain is a huge influence in her work. Elise traveled across South America painting murals in exange for free room and board.
